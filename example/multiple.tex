\section*{Multiple \texttt{\string\wrapgraphics} calls}

Images can be placed multiple times throughout the document.
Each call independently traces the contour and generates
the appropriate \texttt{\string\parshape}.

\subsection*{First image (left)}

\wrapgraphics[width=0.6\textwidth, threshold=0.5]{img/tree.png}
\lipsum[1-2]

\subsection*{Second image (right)}

\wrapgraphics[width=0.6\textwidth, threshold=0.5, position=right]{img/tree.png}
\lipsum[3-6]
